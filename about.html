I am a mathematician and data scientist with 14 years of academic and health sector experience.

I earned my PhD at the University of California, Davis under the supervision of Eric Babson.

From 2015 to 2020, I was an Assistant Professor of Science in the Mathematical, Computational, and Statistical Sciences major at Yale-NUS College in Singapore.

Before joining the faculty at Yale-NUS, I was a research fellow at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm and a postdoc at Aalto University in Helsinki.
