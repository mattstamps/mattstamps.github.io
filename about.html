I am a mathematician with 14 years of academic and healthcare
industry experience and former project lead for locomotion.

I earned my PhD at the University of California, Davis
under the supervision of Eric Babson.

From 2015 to 2020, I was an Assistant Professor of Science
in the Mathematical, Computational, and Statistical Sciences
major at Yale-NUS College in Singapore.

Before joining the faculty at Yale-NUS, I was a postdoc with
Svante Linusson at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology in
Stockholm and Alex Engström at Aalto University in Helsinki.
